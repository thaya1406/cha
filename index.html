Volunteering in Sarawak, Malaysia

ChawanChange VolunTourism Project

A responsible voluntourism project designed in partnership with the Kampung Chawan community to create sustainable, positive change through empowerment, education, and development.

Project Overview

The ChawanChange VolunTourism Project is a unique collaboration between BANTU Sarawak and the residents of Kampung Chawan, an informal settlement near Kuching. For over 30 years, this resilient community of 53 families has strived for a better future. This project moves beyond traditional aid, focusing on a partnership model where international volunteers work alongside community members.

Our mission is built on three pillars: empowering residents through economic opportunities, providing education and exposure for children, and tangibly improving the village through community-led development. You won't just be a visitor; you'll be a partner, a learner, and a catalyst for sustainable, positive change.

The Heart of Our Project: Meet Kampung Chawan

A story of resilience, community, and dreams.

Our Story & People

For over 30 years, Kampung Chawan has been home to 53 families. It's an informal settlement brimming with a powerful 'gotong-royong' (community cooperation) spirit. Despite limited resources, their strength shines through in their skilled hands and their shared motivation to provide a better life for their children.

Our Mission

Empowerment

Fostering economic independence and self-reliance by creating opportunities for the community to earn income through their skills and cultural heritage.

Education

Providing children with valuable skills through engaging STEAM activities and fostering cross-cultural understanding between volunteers and residents.

Development

Tangibly improving the living conditions and shared spaces of the village through collaborative, hands-on construction and clean-up projects.

Your Role as a VolunTourist

Make a real, tangible impact through diverse activities.

Educate & Inspire

Spark curiosity and build confidence in the children of Kampung Chawan. You'll lead engaging sessions in STEAM (Science, Tech, Engineering, Arts, Math), host lively reading circles, get creative with arts & crafts, and organize sports activities. Your energy and knowledge can open up a world of possibilities for them.

Build & Improve

Roll up your sleeves and leave a lasting physical mark. Work side-by-side with community members on vital projects, from repairing and renovating homes to enhancing shared community spaces like playgrounds and gardens. Participate in community-wide cleanups that foster pride and improve health.

Learn & Strategize

Gain a unique insight into the world of social enterprise. Spend time at the BANTU Sarawak office, contributing to administrative tasks, brainstorming new project ideas, and helping us improve our long-term strategy. It's a fantastic opportunity to understand the behind-the-scenes work of community development.

About BANTU Sarawak

BANTU Sarawak is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering local communities through responsible and sustainable initiatives. We believe in building partnerships, not dependencies. Our projects are always co-developed with the communities we serve, ensuring that our efforts are respectful, relevant, and create lasting positive change. We act as a bridge, connecting passionate individuals from around the world with communities that have the vision and drive to create a better future.
